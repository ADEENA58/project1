About Us

Bright World LED Store

At our LED selling light store, we believe that LED technology is the future of lighting. With a market share of only 1.8% in 2012, LED lights have grown to hold more than 50% of the total lighting industry in just 8 years. We expect this trend to continue, with LED lights projected to take around 90% of the market share by 2030. Our store is dedicated to providing high-quality LED lighting products to our customers. We believe that LED lights are the way to go, not just because of their energy efficiency and long lifespan, but also because they are more environmentally friendly than traditional lighting products. We source our products from the best Chinese LED light manufacturers, who have a long history of producing high-quality products. We believe that they offer the best combination of affordability, quality, and fast delivery. Our products range from LED streetlights to LED lamps and bulbs, and we are committed to providing our customers with the best possible products and service. By setting up a retail shop, we aim to provide our customers with a personalized experience, allowing them to see and handle our products in person. This helps them make informed decisions about their purchases and builds trust and loyalty with our brand. We are committed to staying up to date with the latest trends and developments in the LED lighting industry, ensuring that our customers always get the best possible products
